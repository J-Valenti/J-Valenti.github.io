About the Brand

Mystic Bites was created at the height of 2024 as an interactive dinning experience. Our menu is a staple of cafe comfort food with a whimsical feel. Not only do we have our amazing menu but we have an ever changing challenge menu that was the dream behind Mystic Bites.

This restaurant was created to bring a sense of curiosity back to this world! Our renound head chef has created every dish and continues to create to keep our guests guessing and wanting to return.

Once you step foot into Mystic Bites, you will feel an atmosphere filled with lasting curiosity, magic and wonder. Are you willing to be curious like a cat and take a guess?

Our amazing team here at Mystic Bites strides to create an experience like none other and add more magic to the culinary art. Along with adding to the over all culture we want to make sure each customer has an amazing experience, and we want to hold every experience with integrity, quality, and innovation.
