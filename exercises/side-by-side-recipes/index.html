HOMEMADE SPAGHETTI O'S

PREP TIME:

5 MINUTES

COOK TIME:

25 MINUTES

TOTAL TIME:

30 MINUTES

YEILDS:

4

If you grew up loving those little pasta rings in tomato sauce, you know there’s something magically nostalgic about Spaghetti O’s. But honestly? The canned version can feel a little sad compared to what homemade can be. So I set out to recreate that comforting bowl with fresh ingredients, deeper flavors, and a creamy texture that makes you want to savor every bite.
